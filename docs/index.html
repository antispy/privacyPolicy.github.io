This Privacy Policy applies to the Anti-Spy Camera Detector mobile application (hereafter referred to as the "Application"), developed by Alexarix Ltd (hereafter referred to as the "Service Provider") and offered as a freemium utility with optional subscription features. The Application is provided "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect limited information to support core functionality, subscription processing, and usage analytics. This includes:

  • Device type, OS version, and language settings
  • Installation source (e.g., ad campaign ID, referrer)
  • Subscription status and transaction events
  • Anonymous usage metrics (e.g., feature usage, scan counts)
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable scan results (e.g., "a suspicious device was detected in a hotel") to improve our detection algorithms and crowd-sourced safety database.

Privacy Assurance

No sensitive personal data (such as names, email addresses, or specific location coordinates) is collected or stored by the Service Provider. The app's scanning is performed locally on your device.

3. In-App Purchases

Anti-Spy Camera Detector offers optional in-app subscriptions processed by Apple Inc. All payment transactions, billing history, and refunds are handled directly by Apple, not the Service Provider.

Refund requests should be submitted via your Apple account. The Service Provider does not store or access your billing credentials or Apple ID.

4. Local-Only Features & AI Analysis

The following features operate primarily on your device and do not transmit personal data to the Service Provider:

  • IR camera detection via device flashlight
  • Wi-Fi and Bluetooth scanning
  • Magnetometer sensor data for electronic device detection
  • Audio and haptic feedback generation

AI-Powered Analysis: To provide advanced device identification and threat assessment, anonymized device data (such as generic device signatures and network metadata) is sent to our AI analysis service. This data is not linked to you or your specific location and is used solely to classify the type of device (e.g., "smart TV," "router," "suspicious camera") and provide the Smart Security Score. No images, videos, or personal files from your device are ever transmitted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. However, anonymized, aggregated data may be shared with third-party service providers listed above solely to improve app performance and manage subscriptions.

We may disclose limited data if required by law or legal process.

6. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application. You can also limit tracking by:

  • Opting out of ad tracking in your iOS settings
  • Disabling personalized ads via your Apple ID
  • Adjusting Appsflyer opt-out settings here

7. Data Retention

No personal data is stored by the Service Provider. Anonymized analytics and subscription metadata may be retained by our third-party partners (Appsflyer, Adapty) according to their own retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted data, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards (both technical and procedural) to protect any locally processed or third-party managed data. All payment transactions are handled through Apple's secure payment system.
